How to prevent

  1. Wash your hands often using an alcohol based sanitizer
  2. Avoid touching your face eyes nose mouth
  3. Avoid public gatherings,public transport services
  4. Stay home if you are sick
  5. Avoid close contact with people
  6. Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze or use the inside of your elbow.
